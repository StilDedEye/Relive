Privacy Policy – Relive

Last updated: April 25, 2025

Relive respects your privacy. This policy outlines how your data is handled within the app.

1. Data Collected

Relive does not collect or store personal data on external servers. All information, including memories, images, notifications, and settings, is saved exclusively on the user's device.

The app does not require account creation and does not use user tracking or identification systems.

2. Permissions Requested

Relive may request the following permissions to function properly:

  • Location access: used for geolocated capsules (optional feature).
  • Bluetooth access: for Bluetooth capsules (optional feature).
  • Memory/camera access: to allow you to attach images to memories.

These permissions are used exclusively to improve the user experience and are not used to collect personal data.

3. Data Sharing

Relive does not share any data with third parties. No information leaves your device.

4. Security

All data is stored locally. The app does not perform any online backups. We recommend you perform device backups to avoid losing your memories.

5. Changes to the Privacy Policy

Any changes to this policy will be published within the app and/or updated on this page.
