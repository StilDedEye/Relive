THE IDEA

Relive was created to help you preserve your most meaningful moments. Whether it's a trip, a special day, or a memory tied to a place or person, Relive brings back those cherished moments when you need them the most.

TYPES OF CAPSULES

Standard Capsules

Save images and special content for the future. Perfect for keeping meaningful moments and rediscovering them over time.

Geo Capsules

Unlock memories only when you reach a specific location. Make every place a treasure chest of emotions.

Bluetooth Capsules

Capsules that open only when near a specific Bluetooth device. Perfect for creating shared memories with a personal touch.
